Marketing Role

The work reads like a hybrid of operator, builder, and systems thinker.

Using the BLF workspace and related campaign pages as source material, the marketing role is clearly broader than one channel. It includes paid social copy and lead-gen offers, paid search keyword architecture, webinar setup and localization, backlink- aware content planning, and AI-assisted workflow improvement.

The strongest through-line is operational: content is treated as an asset tied to landing pages and campaign goals, while workflow design reduces friction for regional launches. This is industrial B2B marketing built as a system, not just a collection of isolated tasks.
